# Deferred Production Controls

Not implemented in the case-study prototype:
- exact regulatory deadline calculation and notification submission;
- account, payment, wallet, KYC, self-exclusion or regulator integrations;
- attachment OCR, malware scanning, content disarm or biometric processing;
- production RBAC/SSO/MFA, secrets vault, network segmentation and high availability;
- representative production evaluation, drift monitoring and staffed on-call support;
- jurisdiction-specific retention/legal-basis configuration;
- signed model artifacts, SBOM, private package mirror and cryptographic audit chains.

The prototype may output `required_context` keys but does not retrieve those systems.
